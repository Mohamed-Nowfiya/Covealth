PRECAUTIONS

  • Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible.
    Masks are designed to provide an additional layer of protection in situations where you cannot physically distance and in rooms with poor or unknown ventilation.

  • Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.
    Wash your hands thoroughly for minimum 10 seconds.

  • The best way to fight the spread of the virus is to stay at home, be away from your friends and socialise online.
    If you must go outside or meet someone in person make sure you keep a distance of at least 1.5 metre (about 5 feet) between yourself and anyone around.


  • Make sure you, and the people around you, follow good respiratory hygiene.
    This means covering your mouth and nose with your bent elbow or tissue when you cough or sneeze. If you use a tissue to wipe your nose, then dispose it immediately.

  • Do stretches, meditations and yoga to be healthy both physically and mentally.
    Controls anxiety and Enhances self-awareness


  • Eat decent healthy meals and try to have as many vegetables and fruit every day.
    Intake of green vegetables and fibre rich food help in being healthy
