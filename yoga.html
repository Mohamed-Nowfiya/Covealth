Light on the Law of Attraction: A yogic perspective on “The Secret”.

The secret of manifesting the unreal:
First of all, from start to finish “The Secret” affirms-and glorifies- the reality of the physical world (maya). It is fixated on a materialistic form of consumerist bliss. It not-so-subtlety suggests that if we just think happy thoughts we will manifest all the money we want, as many romantic partners as we want, and achieve impeccable health. None of that practice and non-attachment stuff required! From a yogic perspective, the physical world is not ultimate reality. Attempting to find lasting happiness in that which is not real is fruitless. Yoga’s aims are higher. Yoga is both the process of realizing that the physical world is an illusion, and the state of being in union with that which is ultimately real. Yoga is an inward journey. It is not a process that seeks to change our lives in the realm of illusion. It seeks to transform us at our deepest level so that we experience the true and lasting joy that is the only thing that is real. It is a process that requires we learn to see clearly, which means we must first remove the avidya (ignorance) that has been clouding our perspective. The yogic path is one of tapas, devotion and discipline in action.
